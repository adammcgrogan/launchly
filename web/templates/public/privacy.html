Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Who we are

Launchly is operated by Adam McGrogan, based in Belfast, Northern Ireland. We build and host websites for local businesses across Ireland and the UK.

If you have any questions about this policy, email us at hello@launchly.ltd.

What data we collect

When you sign up or get in touch, we collect:

  • —Your name and contact details (email address, phone number)
  • —Your business name, services, and other content you provide for your site
  • —Payment information, processed securely through Stripe (we never store card details ourselves)
  • —Basic usage data if analytics are enabled on your site

When a visitor submits an enquiry through your business site, their name, email, phone number, and message are collected and forwarded to you. We do not use that data for any other purpose.

Why we collect it

We use your data to:

  • —Build and maintain your website
  • —Send you lead notifications and service updates
  • —Process your subscription payments
  • —Respond to any questions or change requests you send us

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not share it with any third party except those listed below who help us deliver the service.

Third parties

We use a small number of trusted services to run Launchly:

  • —Stripe - handles payment processing. Their privacy policy is at stripe.com/privacy.
  • —Resend - used to send transactional emails (lead notifications, site updates).
  • —Railway - hosts the application and database.

How long we keep your data

We keep your account data for as long as you are a customer. If you cancel, we retain it for up to 90 days in case you return, then delete it. You can request immediate deletion at any time by emailing us.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • —Access the personal data we hold about you
  • —Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • —Request deletion of your data
  • —Object to how we process your data

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@launchly.ltd. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Cookies

Launchly.ltd does not use tracking or advertising cookies. If you have analytics enabled on your business site, a privacy-friendly analytics tool (Umami) may set a cookie to count visits. It does not track individuals or share data with third parties.

Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify active customers by email. The date at the top of this page always reflects the most recent update.
