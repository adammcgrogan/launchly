FAQ

Common questions

How quickly will my site be live?
Within 24 hours of you submitting your form, guaranteed. In most cases it's much faster - often the same day.
Do I need any technical knowledge?
None at all. You fill in a simple form with your business details - name, services, phone number - and we handle everything else. Hosting, design, the domain, all of it.
What happens during the 2-week free trial?
Your site goes live immediately and you can share it with customers, start receiving enquiries, and see everything working. We don't ask for a card or any payment details upfront - only once you've seen your site live and are happy to continue. If it's not for you, just say so and you owe nothing.
Can I make changes to my site after it's live?
Yes. Just drop me an email or a text and I'll sort it. You deal directly with me - no support tickets, no waiting on hold. On the Pro plan changes are prioritised and turned around faster.
Is there a contract or minimum term?
No contract, no minimum term. It's a simple monthly subscription. You can cancel any time and your site stays live until the end of the billing period.
